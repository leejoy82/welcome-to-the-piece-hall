History & Heritage

The Colonnades

The stunning three-tiered, open-air galleries are the defining feature of The Piece Hall. The 315 individual rooms that line these colonnades were originally the stalls for the cloth traders. Today, they house a vibrant collection of independent shops, cafes, and creative businesses.
